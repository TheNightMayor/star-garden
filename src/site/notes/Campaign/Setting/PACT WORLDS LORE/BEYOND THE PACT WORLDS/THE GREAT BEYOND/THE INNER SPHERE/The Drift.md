### The Drift 
Reachable only via technology, the Drift allows starships to quickly travel great distances. A relatively new plane, the Drift is generally considered to be part of the Inner Sphere. However, due to its strangeness, scholars are still debating how it fits into the grand design. For information on the Drift, see page 290.
