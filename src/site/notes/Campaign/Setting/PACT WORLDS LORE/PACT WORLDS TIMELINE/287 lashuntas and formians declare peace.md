# Lashuntas and Formians Declare Peace
Castrovel’s lashuntas and formians sign a landmark peace deal, ending millennia of conflict.
Though the insectile formians battled with lashuntas for millennia, the coming of the Pact and careful shirren-brokered peace talks finally ended hostilities a mere 30 years ago. Today, the formian hives known as the Colonies work together to farm the land and trade with other races, with each individual hive queen paying tribute to the Overqueen—a purely philosophical entity established during the historic Meeting of Queens

Shirrens broker a peace deal between lashuntas and formians on Castrovel, bringing an end to thousands of years of war between the two peoples. This event is believed to have been necessary for the alliance forged between the Veskarium and the Pact World government in 291 AG.
