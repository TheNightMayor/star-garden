The Starfinder Society under First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo faces a coordinated media smear campaign.
First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo steps down following the election of Ehu Hadif as her replacement.
In 319 AG, AbadarCorp employee Datch machinated a smear campaign against the Starfinder Society. Secretly a character assassin and devotee of 
Lao Shu Po, her true target was AbadarCorp’s leadership.
