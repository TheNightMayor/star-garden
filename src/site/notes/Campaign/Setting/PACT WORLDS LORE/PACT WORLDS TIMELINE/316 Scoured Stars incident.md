# Scoured Stars


## 316-319 AG
### First Seeker Jadnura
The Starfinder Society elects the kasatha explorer Jadnura as its First Seeker.

First Seeker Jadnura leads a task force of approximately 80% of all Starfinder Society assets into the Scoured Stars system. The task force is cut off from the rest of the galaxy in the Scoured Stars Incident.

### The Incident
First Seeker Jadnura coordinated an expedition to the Scoured Stars, an uncharted region of space. Upon their arrival, an impenetrable energy shield encapsulated the entire system, preventing communication and travel. Dubbed the Godshield, this barrier trapped 80% of the Society’s agents and assets in the dangerous system. Concurrently, the Starfinder bastion of Sangoro’s Bulwark was conquered by a vicious battalion of grioth invaders.

### Elsebo Acting First Seeker
The Scoured Stars incident left the Society on the brink of ruin. The organization survived thanks to the leadership of First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo. Luwazi hired reputable mercenary organizations to keep the Society running long enough for a new crop of Starfinders to complete their training. New agents spent their first year working to rebuild the Society.

## 317 AG
### The Rescue
The Starfinder Society, led by Acting First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo, mounts a rescue mission for the Starfinders marooned by the Scoured Stars Incident. A few months into 318 AG, they successfully rescued First Seeker Jadnura and other Starfinders trapped in the Scoured Stars. 

## 318 AG
The Starfinder Society, led by joint First Seekers Jadnura and Luwazi Elsebo, combats the Jinsul menace unleashed following the rescue mission that responded to the Scoured Stars Incident. 

In 318 AG, Luwazi Elsebo launched an expedition to rescue survivors of the Scoured Stars incident, tangling with xenophobic jinsuls. The long conflict culminated in the defeat of a heretical jinsul leader and the reawakening of the god Kadrical, who slumbered at the heart of the Scoured Stars. Jadnura chose to become caretaker of the system, leaving Luwazi as First Seeker.

## 319 AG
### Elsebo Steps Down
The Starfinder Society under First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo faces a coordinated media smear campaign.
First Seeker Luwazi Elsebo steps down following the election of Ehu Hadif as her replacement.

In 319 AG, AbadarCorp employee Datch machinated a smear campaign against the Starfinder Society. Secretly a character assassin and devotee of Lao Shu Po, her true target was AbadarCorp’s leadership.

### Kadrical Awakens 
The god Kadrical awakens in the heart of the Scoured Stars after a long sleep.
