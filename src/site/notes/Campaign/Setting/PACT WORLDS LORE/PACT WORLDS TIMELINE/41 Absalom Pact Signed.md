After their victory at Aledra, the worlds of the Golarion System united. The Absalom Pact was signed in 41 AG, joining the independent planets into the Pact Worlds, a coalition pledged to mutual defense and governance. Strangely, dread Eox was the first power to sign the Pact. Thereafter, the Golarion System became known as the Pact System
