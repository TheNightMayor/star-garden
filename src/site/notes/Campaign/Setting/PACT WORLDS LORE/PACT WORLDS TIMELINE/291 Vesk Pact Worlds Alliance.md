The Swarm attacks the Pact Worlds system, prompting a defensive alliance between the Pact Worlds system and the Veskarium and a ceasefire in the more than 200 year-old Silent War. 

The Aspis Consortium, a massive criminal conglomerate, brokered this alliance and now has the equivalent of Pact Worlds status itself on the strength of that achievement.
