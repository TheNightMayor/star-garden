during the Stardust Plague, public opinion turned against the Society, beginning a century-long “Time of Uncertainty.” During these tumultuous years, First Seeker Hilbrin expanded the Lorespire Complex and acquired the technology required to create Guidance, and First Seeker Lanrah promised to solve the Gap. Lanrah’s tenure was so disastrous, future First Seekers were forever banned from choosing Gap-related mandates. After Lanrah died during the Battle of the Amethyst Transcendence in 160 AG, her successor, First Seeker Lezosk, spent his term building a secret training ground known as “Lezosk’s Perplexity.” The Perplexity was sealed after Lezosk’s mysterious disappearance in 168 AG—the doors open once every year, and elite Starfinders are dispatched on missions within. With Lezosk’s disappearance, the Time of Uncertainty came to an end and the Society rebounded, entering a long period of prosperity and stability.
