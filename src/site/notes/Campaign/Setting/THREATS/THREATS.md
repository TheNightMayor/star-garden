# THREATS
The galaxy is a dangerous place. Even within the Pact Worlds, threats lurk around every corner, and with unlimited opportunity comes unlimited risk. While every religion, faction, and corporation is a threat to someone, the following section details six of the most notorious, system-wide threats— powerful enemies capable of destroying or enslaving the Pact Worlds as a whole. From the locust-like Swarm to the tyrannical Azlanti Star Empire, these are entities that can make even the bitterest of foes join forces in mutual defense
