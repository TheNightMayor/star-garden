### Skyfire Legion 
Based on Triaxus, the Skyfire Legion is a peculiarly principled mercenary group that sells protective services primarily to colonists and other groups operating outside the Pact Worlds, where the Stewards can’t directly help them. Like their famed antecedents, the dragonriders of Triaxus’s ancient Dragon Legion, many Skyfire legionnaires form an almost mystical bond with Triaxian dragonkin, and the best Skyfire attack ships are piloted by a pair of legionnaires: a humanoid pilot and her bonded dragonkin copilot. Although the majority of Skyfire legionnaires are native ryphorians, membership is open to all races, and androids, humans, lashuntas, and vesk have all joined the ranks of the Skyfire Legion.
