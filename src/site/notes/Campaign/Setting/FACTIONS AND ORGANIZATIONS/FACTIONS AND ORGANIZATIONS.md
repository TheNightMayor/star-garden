# FACTIONS AND ORGANIZATIONS 
The Pact Worlds are a riot of competing interests, from planetary governments and churches to big business and organized crime. Different cultures, organizations, and species are constantly working together or locked in violent opposition, or some combination of the two, and who you know often makes the difference between fame, fortune, and farming protein grubs on some backwater colony world.
